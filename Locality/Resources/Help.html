Welcome to Locality.

Setting up Locality

1. Your initial profile

Click the default profile, Default and then rename it to your current location, for example, Home. Click Near a location, click choose, select your current location on the map and click Save location Return to the main screen by clicking Back button.

2. Creating a second profile

Now click the plus button to create a new profile. Name it after another location, such as Work. Click Near a location, click choose, select your current location on the map and click Save location Click Apply to select this profile. Now you can change your PC settings for this Work profile. Locality will remember your previous settings in Home profile automatically.
Locality will also activate the relevant profile when you move the device into proximity of the specified profile location.

More

You can configure Locality to activate a profile based on available Wi-Fi networks, or launch a selected application when a profile is activated.
